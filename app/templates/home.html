Actor Matching Game

Search for a movie, match actors to their pictures, see your score!




To Begin: Enter the title of a movie into the search bar and hit Start the Game! Capitalization and spacing do not matter but do please try and spell the movie correctly. If you are having trouble with finding a movie to play with, click here to see all the movies this game supports

Supported Movies

  • Despicable Me
  • Top Gun
  • Pride & Prejudice
  • The Avengers
  • Inception
Directions: Match the actors with their picture. Click and drag an actors name over a picture and drop. When you are finished matching, click Check Your Answers to see your score. Once you drop a name on to a picture you cannot change it, so be careful matching. If you do make a mistake, simply click the Reset button and the game will be reset. If you would like to play with a different movie click Chose Another Movie, and you will be taken back to the screen where you can chose a movie.
